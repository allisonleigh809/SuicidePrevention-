Resources

Racial and queer/trans trauma is real, and a threat to your mental health. The statistics for POC (people of color) and the LGBTQ+ communities to seek help is less likely to happen. However, at the end of every storm there is sunshine and healing is possible, and it's important to treat yourself with love, respect, and compassion. These resources are focused specifically for POC and the LGBTQ+ mental health if you are looking for threapy and support. The resources and organizations are ready to help and heal.
